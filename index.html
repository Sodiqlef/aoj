About Us

Excellence in financial services since 1998

AOJ Professional Services (Chartered Accountants) is a medium size audit, advisory, training and tax services firm established to help businesses create value for their customers. The emphasis of our work is on practical solutions to enhance business success.

We offer assurance and related consulting services, bookkeeping and accountancy, strategic business and investment consultancy, Information Technology and Security Services, In-Business Courses, In-House Training and One-to-One Executive Coaching.

The firm started financial training and consulting operations in February 1998 and was formally renamed AOJ Professional Services in 2008.

Our Vision & Mission

It is our VISION to emerge among top three Small and Medium-Sized Audit Practitioners in Nigeria by "PROVIDING EXCELLENT BUSINESS SERVICES THAT ADD VALUE TO OUR CLIENTS"

We provide the most reliable and responsible business support services to our clients and are committed partners towards their growth and continuing success.

Joseph Nosa

Principal Consultant | FCA

Joseph Nosa has over 25 years of experience in Audit, Financial Consulting Management Development. He has a passionate belief that people are the key to success in any organization.

Joseph is a Fellow of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of Nigeria (FCA). He has also attended many international trainings in financial management. He is an alumnus of London Business School and University of Northampton, UK. He had public accountancy practice in Johnson and Associates, and JBA and Co for many years before moving to the banking industry. While in banking industry, he worked in various areas including Risk Management, Bad Loan Work Out and Business Restructuring, Corporate Banking, Operations, Retail Sales, Property Management, Project Marketing, Corporate Governance, Compliance, and Internal Audit.

Joseph has a passion for enabling people to learn in a more sustainable training approach. He is a dynamic, enthusiastic, and energetic facilitator and trainer. He believes in always working for the interest of the clients and yet is also able to challenge sensitively to lead change where appropriate.

Fidelis Akaeze
