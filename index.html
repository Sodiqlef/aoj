About Us

AOJ Professional Services (Chartered Accountants) is a medium size audit, advisory, training and tax services firm established to help businesses create value for their customers. The emphasis of our work is on practical solutions to enhance business success.

We offer assurance and related consulting services, bookkeeping and accountancy, strategic business and investment consultancy, Information Technology and Security Services, In-Business Courses, In-House Training and One-to-One Executive Coaching.

The firm started financial training and consulting operations in February 1998 and was formally renamed AOJ Professional Services in 2008.
